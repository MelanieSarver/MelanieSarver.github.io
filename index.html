Bio

I'm a brand-new, shiny software developer, who has recently graduated from Codeup in San Antonio, TX. As they say, "I wasn't born in Texas, but I got here as fast as I could!" I was brought here for the first time by my husband's Army career, and now, several moves later, we are back and here to stay! I'm very excited to put down some real roots and start writing my new story.

The purpose of this page is for you to get to know a little bit about me by checking out some of the projects that I've been working on. Some of the work you'll see is complete, and some of it is ongoing work in progress. I had virtually no coding experience a few months ago. If you'd like to see how I'm growing and changing, go poke around my GitHub!

Front-end || Back-end is a good question that I don't have a simple answer to. Five months ago my answer was whole-heartedly front-end. After all, I had the perseverance to get a Bachelor's in Graphic Design, so what else could I be? Then, I met Java, and found that I'm surprisingly attracted to the logic.
